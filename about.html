Building the Future of Responsible Mining

Founded on principles of excellence, sustainability, and community partnership, Hubris Minerals is transforming the gold mining landscape in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Our Story

Established in 2015, Hubris Minerals emerged from a vision to revolutionize gold mining practices in the Democratic Republic of Congo. What began as a small operation has grown into one of the region's most respected mining companies.

Our journey started with a simple yet powerful belief: that mining operations can and should benefit everyone involved ‚Äì from investors and employees to local communities and the environment. This philosophy has guided every decision we've made and continues to shape our future.

Today, we operate twelve active mining sites across the DRC, employing over 2,000 people directly and supporting thousands more through our community development programs. Our commitment to ethical practices has earned us recognition from industry leaders and regulatory bodies worldwide.
